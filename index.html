copperexport by zzamboni

Coppermine iPhoto export plugin

CopperExport for iPhoto is a plugin for iPhoto 4 through 7 which allows you to export pictures directly into a Coppermine gallery. It works with Coppermine 1.3.x and 1.4.x. Click on the screenshot to the right to see a larger version. I would love to hear from you if you find it useful, if you find something that does not work properly, or if you have any other comments or suggestions. See the email address at the bottom of this document, or visit the forums. See the ChangeLog below for a detailed list of changes.
